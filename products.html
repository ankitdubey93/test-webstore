Our Standards

Every Material Certified

We never use conventional cotton, synthetic dyes, or virgin polyester. Here's what goes into every Lumara piece.

🌿

GOTS Organic Cotton

Global Organic Textile Standard certified — no pesticides, no synthetic fertilisers.

🐑

Responsible Wool Standard

All wool sourced from RWS-certified farms that prioritise animal welfare and land management.

♻️

Recycled Synthetics

Bluesign-certified recycled polyester and ECONYL nylon sourced from post-consumer waste.
