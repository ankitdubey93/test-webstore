Our Story

Started in a Garage, Built on Principles

In 2015, founder Maya Osei started Lumara Apparel from her garage in Portland with a single sewing machine, three bolts of organic cotton, and an unshakeable belief that fashion could be both beautiful and ethical.

Today, Lumara operates three fully owned factories across Portugal, India, and Peru — each paying fair wages, using renewable energy, and zero discharge processes. We serve customers across 28 countries who want clothing they can feel good about wearing.
